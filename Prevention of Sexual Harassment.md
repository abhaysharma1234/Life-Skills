## 1) What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Touching someone without their permission.
- Asking or forcing someone for sexual favors.
- Making sexual jokes or comments about someone’s body or looks.
- Sending or showing dirty messages, photos, or videos.
- Asking someone again and again to go out even after they say no.
- Looking at someone in a wrong way or making sexual signs with hands or eyes.
- Giving threats or promising rewards to get sexual favors.

---

## 2) What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

- Tell the person clearly to stop.
- Write down what happened and keep proof if possible.
- Inform your manager, HR, or the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC).
- Help and support the person who is affected.
- If no action is taken, complain to higher authorities.
