# Listening and Active Communication

### Question 1
What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? (Minimum 6 points)
### Answer 1
The steps and strategies to actively listen are as follows: 
* Avoid getting distracted by your own thoughts.
* Focus on the speaker and topic
* Don't interrupt the other person.
* Let the other person finish and then respond.
* Use body language to show that you are listening.
* Takes notes during important conversations.

### Question 2
According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening? (Write in your own words, use simple English)
### Answer 2
The key points are as follows:
* Mirror Emotions
* Understand the meaning
* Encourage the speaker
* Be patient and calm
* Ask clarifying questions

### Question 3
What are the obstacles in your listening process?
### Answer 3
I always try to listen others carefully but sometimes distractions like noise or phone, daydreaming, emotions, and interruptions sometimes reduce my focus and make it harder to understand others.

### Question 4
What can you do to improve your listening?
### Answer 4
I can improve my listening by removing distractions, staying calm, and focusing fully on the speaker. I can try to be patient, avoid interrupting the speaker, ask clear questions, take small notes, and think before responding.

### Question 5
When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?
### Answer 5
I switch to a passive communication style when listening is more important than speaking, such as during feedback, small conflicts, or sensitive situations. I also become passive with seniors or when unsure, to better understand others’ views.

### Question 6
When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?
### Answer 6
I usually try to stay calm and respectful, but sometimes I become aggressive when I am very tired, stressed, or someone keeps disturbing me again and again. In urgent situations, I try to speak politely and directly to solve problems quickly.

### Question 7
When do you switch into Passive Aggressive (sarcasm/gossiping/taunts/silent treatment and others) communication styles in your day to day life?
### Answer 7
I try to avoid passive-aggressive behavior and prefer honest communication. But sometimes, when I feel afraid, uncomfortable, or unable to speak directly, I may use silence, sarcasm, or indirect words to show my feelings instead of clearly expressing them.
